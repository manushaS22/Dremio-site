    Rise Of The Damned

    "Rise of the Damned" is a gripping action-horror film set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a global pandemic, transforming people into savage and bloodthirsty creatures. Amidst the chaos, a courageous young woman emerges as the reluctant leader of a group of survivors. They must navigate through desolate landscapes and confront both the relentless undead and the darkness within themselves. The film weaves a tale of survival, sacrifice, and the unbreakable will to live, showcasing heart-pounding action and spine-chilling suspense. As they fight to retain their humanity and overcome the horrors around them, "Rise of the Damned" delivers a thrilling and intense cinematic experience that keeps audiences on the edge of their seats until the very end.

    Horror | Mystery | Sci-Fi | 2023 | USA | 1 hour 52 min
