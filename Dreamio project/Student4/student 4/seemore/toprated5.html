However, what was supposed to be a peaceful voyage turns into a labyrinth of deceit, betrayal, and murder when a shocking crime takes place onboard. As Poirot unravels the web of secrets and hidden motives among the eclectic group of passengers, tensions rise, and everyone becomes a suspect. "Death on the Nile" is a gripping and visually stunning cinematic experience that pays homage to Christie's timeless storytelling and keeps the legacy of the world's greatest detective alive.
