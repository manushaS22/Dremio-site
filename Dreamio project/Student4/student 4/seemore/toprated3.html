    The King's Man

    "The King's Man" is an exhilarating and action-packed spy thriller that serves as a prequel to the beloved "Kingsman" film series. Set during the backdrop of World War I, the movie unveils the origins of the elite and independent intelligence agency known as the Kingsman. As the world faces the threat of criminal masterminds plotting chaos and destruction, a group of courageous individuals comes together under the guidance of a charismatic mentor to form the Kingsman agency. Fueled by heart-stopping action, clever humor, and a riveting storyline, "The King's Man" takes the audience on a thrilling journey through espionage, dangerous missions, and the creation of an iconic spy organization.
