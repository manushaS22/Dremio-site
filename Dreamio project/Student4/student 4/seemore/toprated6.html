    THE ADAM PROJECT

    "The Adam Project" is an action-packed and mind-bending sci-fi film that follows a man named Adam Reed, portrayed by Ryan Reynolds, who travels back in time to seek help from his 13-year-old self, played by Walker Scobell. The mission is to save his father, a brilliant scientist, from a fatal encounter with a malevolent organization bent on altering the fabric of reality. As the father and son duo team up across time, they are joined by a formidable fighter played by Zoe Saldana, and a skilled pilot portrayed by Mark Ruffalo. Together, they embark on an adrenaline-fueled adventure, battling futuristic adversaries and confronting emotional complexities. With a seamless blend of action, humor, and heart, "The Adam Project" explores themes of family, destiny, and the power of second chances, delivering an exhilarating cinematic journey that captivates audiences with its imaginative storytelling and dynamic performances.

    Horror | Mystery | Sci-Fi | 2023 | USA | 1 hour 52 min
