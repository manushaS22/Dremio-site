    Bird Box Barcelona

    "Bird Box Barcelona" is a captivating drama film set in the bustling city of Barcelona. The story follows the journey of a young artist who, after losing her sight in a tragic accident, discovers a newfound ability to perceive the world through her art. Guided by her unwavering determination, she embarks on an extraordinary adventure, challenging societal norms and inspiring those around her to see beyond the confines of their own perceptions. As she navigates the labyrinthine streets of the city, she unravels the interconnectedness of human emotions and the beauty of life's unseen wonders. "Bird Box Barcelona" is a visually stunning and emotionally gripping film that explores the power of resilience and the transformative nature of art.

    Horror | Mystery | Sci-Fi | 2023 | USA | 1 hour 52 min
