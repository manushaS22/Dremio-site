    Takkar

    "Takkar" is a compelling drama film set in the bustling city of Mumbai. The story revolves around a talented street dancer who, in a tragic turn of events, becomes paralyzed from the waist down. Faced with seemingly insurmountable challenges, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. Drawing strength from his passion for dance and music, he defies societal norms and sets out to prove that one's spirit can triumph over physical limitations. As he navigates the diverse and vibrant streets of Mumbai, he unravels the interconnectedness of human emotions and the hidden potential within each individual. "Takkar" is an emotionally gripping and visually captivating film that celebrates the power of determination, the unyielding spirit of the human soul, and the transformative nature of art.

    Horror | Mystery | Sci-Fi | 2023 | USA | 1 hour 52 min
